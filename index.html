How Inspection AI Works

Inspection AI transforms traditional HACCP food safety routines into structured digital workflows. Staff complete safety checks on mobile devices while the platform verifies evidence, detects deviations, and produces inspection-ready compliance reports.

01

Digital HACCP Tasks

Daily food safety tasks such as temperature checks, cleaning verification, delivery inspections, and allergen controls are scheduled automatically. Staff receive clear instructions through a mobile checklist to ensure consistent execution during every shift.

02

Evidence Capture

When a task is completed, the system records structured evidence including photos, temperature readings, timestamps, staff identity, and device information. This creates a verifiable record that proves checks were completed correctly.

03

Deviation Detection

If a safety limit is breached, the platform automatically detects the deviation and launches corrective action workflows. Staff are guided through required steps such as re-checks, stock isolation, or manager approval.

04

Compliance & Inspection Reports

Inspection AI generates chronological compliance reports containing completed checks, incidents, corrective actions, and approvals. Managers can instantly export inspection-ready evidence packs for regulators or auditors.

Inspection AI Platform

Inspection AI is a mobile-first Food Safety Management System that turns HACCP compliance into structured digital workflows. Staff complete safety tasks through guided mobile checklists while the platform captures verifiable evidence, detects deviations, and generates inspection-ready compliance documentation.

Mobile HACCP Task Engine

Digitised daily food safety routines for kitchen teams.

Inspection AI converts HACCP and PRP procedures into role-based mobile tasks such as temperature checks, cleaning verification, delivery inspections, and allergen controls. Staff complete structured checklists during each shift while managers monitor completion status through real-time dashboards.

Evidence Capture System

Verifiable compliance proof recorded at source.

Each completed task captures structured evidence including photos, temperature readings, timestamps, device identity, and staff credentials. This evidence is automatically linked to the task record to create a defensible compliance trail for inspections and audits.

Deviation & Incident Engine

Automated detection of food safety failures.

When a safety limit is breached, the platform automatically opens a deviation incident. Guided corrective action playbooks lead staff through required steps such as re-checks, stock isolation, disposal decisions, and manager approvals to ensure consistent responses.

Inspection Evidence Packs

One-click compliance reporting for auditors.

Inspection AI automatically generates chronological evidence packs showing completed checks, incidents, corrective actions, and manager approvals. These inspection-ready reports help businesses demonstrate due diligence during regulatory visits.

AI Proof Verification

AI ensures safety evidence is valid and reliable.

On-device computer vision verifies that photos contain required objects such as probe displays, sanitiser labels, or equipment markers before a task can be completed. This reduces fake or incomplete evidence and improves compliance reliability.

Risk Analytics & Dashboards

Identify compliance risks before inspections occur.

The platform analyses task completion patterns, recurring deviations, missed checks and training gaps to calculate site-level risk indicators. Managers receive dashboards and alerts highlighting potential compliance vulnerabilities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn how Inspection AI helps hospitality businesses digitise HACCP food safety routines, automate compliance monitoring, and produce inspection-ready evidence using a modern Food Safety Management System.

What is Inspection AI?

Inspection AI is a mobile-first Food Safety Management System (FSMS) that digitises HACCP monitoring tasks such as temperature checks, cleaning routines, allergen controls, and delivery inspections. Staff complete tasks using a mobile device while the platform captures time-stamped evidence and generates inspection-ready compliance records.

How does Inspection AI improve food safety compliance?

The platform converts paper-based safety logs into structured digital workflows. Tasks are scheduled for staff, evidence is captured through photos and data inputs, and deviations automatically trigger corrective action workflows with manager approval.

What types of checks can the platform monitor?

Inspection AI supports multiple HACCP routines including temperature checks, cleaning verification, allergen control procedures, delivery inspection logs, and equipment safety monitoring across daily kitchen operations.

How does the platform handle food safety deviations?

When a safety limit is exceeded, the system automatically opens a corrective-action workflow. Staff follow guided steps such as rechecking equipment, isolating affected food items, and recording actions until a manager approves and closes the incident.

What is an inspection-ready evidence pack?

Inspection AI can generate a complete evidence pack that includes chronological records of safety checks, corrective actions, manager approvals, and photo evidence. This allows businesses to demonstrate due diligence quickly during health inspections.

Who should use Inspection AI?

Inspection AI is designed for restaurants, takeaways, cafes, hospitality SMEs, and food safety consultants who want to digitise their HACCP compliance processes and maintain consistent operational food safety standards.
